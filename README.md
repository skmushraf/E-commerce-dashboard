📊 Ecommerce Sales Analysis Dashboard
A Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize key performance metrics of an e-commerce business. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, profitability, customer payment preferences, product category contributions, regional sales distribution, monthly trends, and top-performing products. It enables quick decision-making through interactive and visually intuitive reports.

📌 Dashboard Overview
The Ecommerce Sales Analysis Dashboard presents a comprehensive view of business performance with a total sales value of ₹49.38M and a total profit of ₹7.38M. It highlights:
Sales and profit KPIs for overall business performance.
Distribution of orders across different payment methods.
Sales contribution from product categories.
State-wise sales comparison to identify high-performing regions.
Monthly sales trends for seasonality analysis.
Top 10 products generating the highest revenue.

🔑 Key Highlights
1. Business Performance
Total Sales: ₹49.38 Million
Total Profit: ₹7.38 Million
2. State-wise Sales Analysis
Rajasthan recorded the highest sales (~₹7.2M).
Karnataka and Gujarat closely followed with approximately ₹7.1M each.
Sales are relatively balanced among the top-performing states.

🛠 Tools Used

Data Modeling – Relationship management and analysis.

🚀 Project Goal
The primary goal of this dashboard is to transform raw e-commerce transaction data into meaningful insights that help stakeholders understand business performance, identify growth opportunities, and make informed strategic decisions.
